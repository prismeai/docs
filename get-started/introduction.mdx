## What is Prisme.ai?

**Prisme.ai** is an **enterprise-grade, cloud-agnostic Generative AI platform** designed to accelerate AI adoption across your organization—securely, scalably, and efficiently.

Built for enterprises of all sizes and maturities, Prisme.ai provides a modular foundation to deploy, orchestrate, and govern GenAI use cases tailored to business needs.

### Key Capabilities

- **Secure, Enterprise-Ready Infrastructure**  
  Built with compliance, data privacy, and enterprise-scale reliability in mind. Designed to meet the highest security standards across cloud and hybrid environments.

- **From No-Code to Full-Code Interfaces**  
  Empower all user profiles—from business users to developers—with intuitive interfaces. Create, customize, and deploy AI agents effortlessly with visual tools or code as needed.

-  **Multi-LLM Compatibility**  
  Natively supports leading large language models (LLMs), allowing you to choose the best model for each use case while keeping control over data and cost.

-  **AI SecureChat**  
  A ChatGPT-like experience, secured for enterprise use. Enables document-based interactions, task execution, and collaborative canvas-based workspaces—ideal for both individual productivity and expert workflows.

- **Knowledge Activation with RAG, Text-to-SQL & Tools Integration**  
  Tap into your organizational knowledge with Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), structured data access through Text-to-SQL, and seamless tool integrations for business operations.

- **Advanced Orchestration for AI Workflows**  
  Build, connect, and scale complex agent-based workflows. Orchestrate interactions between multiple AI agents, systems, and business tools to automate and augment enterprise processes.
