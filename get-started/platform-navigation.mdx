The Prisme.ai platform offers a comprehensive set of features and products, organized in an intuitive interface. This guide will help you understand how to navigate the platform effectively and find what you need.

## Main Dashboard

When you log in to Prisme.ai, you'll land on the main dashboard, which serves as your central hub for accessing all platform features.

  <img src="/images/dashboard.png" alt="Prisme.ai main dashboard" />

The dashboard includes:

2. **Product Navigation** - Quick access to all Prisme.ai products depending on your role
3. **Recent Activity** - Recently accessed agents and news
4. **Quick Stats** - Usage statistics and performance metrics
5. **Featured Content** - Highlighted resources and new features

## Navigation Structure

Prisme.ai's navigation is organized into several key areas:

### Side Navigation Menu

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/dashboard-nav.png" alt="Top navigation bar" />
</Frame>

The side navigation bar includes:

- **Logo** - Click to return to the dashboard
- **Home** - Global home across the platform, and Access system notifications and
- **AI Secure Chat** - Your organization Secure Chat Agent
- **Products** - Your organization products depending on your ole
- **User** - Access your account, help, feedback, releases note

## Product Navigation

Each Prisme.ai product has its own dedicated navigation and interface. Here's a quick overview of how to navigate each product:
