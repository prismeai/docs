### Initial Foundation Training (2 Days)

This introductory course provides a comprehensive overview of Prisme.ai's platform, features, and basic implementation. It's the ideal starting point for all team members involved in your Prisme.ai journey.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Day 1: Platform Introduction and Core Concepts">
    **Morning Session:**
    - Introduction to Prisme.ai architecture
    - Understanding the AI product ecosystem
    - Getting started with AI Knowledge
    - Basic prompt engineering principles
    
    **Afternoon Session:**
    - Creating your first RAG agent
    - Document management and knowledge bases
    - Testing and validating AI responses
    - Best practices for deployment
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Day 2: Building and Managing AI Solutions">
    **Morning Session:**
    - AI Store fundamentals
    - Agent deployment and management
    - Integration options overview
    - Security and governance basics
    
    **Afternoon Session:**
    - Introduction to AI Builder
    - Creating simple workflows
    - Monitoring and analytics
    - Collaborative implementation planning
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

**Who Should Attend**: Project managers, business analysts, IT decision-makers, and anyone who will be working with the Prisme.ai platform.

**Prerequisites**: None

**Outcome**: Participants will have a solid understanding of Prisme.ai's capabilities and be able to contribute to implementation planning and basic usage.

### Advanced Technical Training (2 Days)

This in-depth course focuses on the technical aspects of implementing, extending, and optimizing Prisme.ai. It covers advanced development techniques, integration strategies, and performance tuning.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Day 1: Advanced Development and Integration">
    **Morning Session:**
    - Deep dive into Prisme.ai architecture
    - API integration strategies
    - Customizing and extending AI agents
    - Advanced prompt engineering
    
    **Afternoon Session:**
    - Building custom applications with AI Builder
    - Webhook implementation and management
    - Authentication and security controls
    - Event-driven architecture patterns
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Day 2: Optimization, Scaling, and DevOps">
    **Morning Session:**
    - Performance optimization techniques
    - Scaling strategies for high-volume deployments
    - Monitoring and observability
    - Troubleshooting methodologies
    
    **Afternoon Session:**
    - CI/CD integration
    - Deployment automation
    - Version control best practices
    - Advanced governance and compliance
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

**Who Should Attend**: Developers, system administrators, DevOps engineers, technical architects, and other IT professionals who will be implementing and managing Prisme.ai.

**Prerequisites**: Completion of Initial Foundation Training or equivalent experience; familiarity with web technologies, APIs, and software development concepts.

**Outcome**: Participants will be able to implement advanced Prisme.ai solutions, integrate with existing systems, optimize performance, and manage the platform throughout its lifecycle.

### Super User Training (1/2 Day)

This focused training prepares business users to create, manage, and optimize AI agents without requiring deep technical expertise. It's ideal for subject matter experts who need to build specialized agents for their domains.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Half-Day Intensive Program">
    **Session Overview:**
    - The role of AI agents in business processes
    - Building effective knowledge bases
    - Agent creation without coding
    - Testing and refining AI responses
    - Prompt crafting for business contexts
    - Managing and updating agents
    - Collaborative agent development
    - Performance monitoring and improvement
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

**Who Should Attend**: Business analysts, knowledge managers, subject matter experts, department leads, and other non-technical stakeholders who will be creating and managing AI agents.

**Prerequisites**: Basic familiarity with Prisme.ai (introduction or demo).

**Outcome**: Participants will be able to independently create, test, and manage AI agents that leverage their domain expertise, without requiring developer assistance.
