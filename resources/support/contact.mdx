## Response Times

| Priority | Standard | Professional | Enterprise |
|----------|----------|-------------|------------|
| Critical (platform down) | 4 hours | 2 hours | 1 hour |
| High (feature broken) | 8 hours | 4 hours | 2 hours |
| Medium (degraded) | 24 hours | 12 hours | 4 hours |
| Low (question) | 48 hours | 24 hours | 8 hours |

Business hours: 9 AM – 6 PM CET, Monday–Friday.

Enterprise customers have access to 24/7 emergency support.

## Filing a Support Request

Include the following for faster resolution:

1. **Environment** — SaaS or self-hosted (version, cloud provider)
2. **Reproduction steps** — Exact steps to reproduce the issue
3. **Correlation ID** — The `x-correlation-id` from error responses
4. **Expected vs. actual behavior**
5. **Logs** — Relevant pod logs or browser console output
6. **Screenshots** — If applicable

## Escalation Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Level 1 — Support Team">

  <Step title="Level 2 — Engineering">
    Bug investigation, root cause analysis, workaround development.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Level 3 — Core Team">
    Critical issues requiring code changes or architectural decisions.

</Steps>
