AI Insights provides real-time analytics to measure how AI agents perform across departments and use cases.  
It helps business leaders and AI teams understand impact, optimize adoption, and track return on investment (ROI).

---

## Business Context

For enterprises deploying dozens or hundreds of AI agents, measuring performance is essential to drive improvement and governance.  
AI Insights brings transparency through unified dashboards, usage metrics, and evaluation frameworks.

It allows teams to:
- Quantify AI adoption and activity levels.  
- Evaluate model performance and user satisfaction.  
- Identify opportunities for optimization and automation.
