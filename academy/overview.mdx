## How to Use This Academy

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start with AI Secure Chat">
    Discover how to use your first secure, multi-LLM chat agent.
    Learn how to handle private data safely, collaborate with teammates, and generate compliant, high-quality answers.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore AI Store">
    Browse, create, and publish AI agents across your organization.
    Learn how to configure visibility, permissions, and categories to manage your internal AI catalog efficiently.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build with AI Knowledge">
    Turn your enterprise knowledge — from documents and APIs — into intelligent agents.\
    Learn how to create agents, configure evaluations, and track analytics to continuously improve performance.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Govern  with AI Governance">
    Control access, ensure compliance, and monitor agents across all your lines of business (LoBs).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Orchestrate with AI Builder">
    Connect agents, tools, and APIs into automated workflows.
    Learn how to design autonomous agents or end-to-end AI pipelines that support complex business operations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Measure with AI Insights">
    Track AI performance, adoption, and ROI across every agent — empowering your organization to scale AI responsibly and efficiently.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Extend with AI Collection">
    Enable your agents to query and enrich structured data
    Learn how to build text-to-SQL experiences and integrate real-time data sources for decision-making.
  </Step>

</Steps>
