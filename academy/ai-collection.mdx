AI Collection enables enterprises to connect and enrich **structured data** from internal databases, APIs, or business systems.  
It powers text-to-SQL agents, real-time analytics, and intelligent data workflows — allowing business users to query complex datasets conversationally.

---

## Business Context

Structured data is one of the most valuable yet underused assets in large organizations.  
AI Collection bridges this gap by connecting AI agents to relational and analytical data sources through secure, governed access.

It enables:
- Secure querying of enterprise datasets.  
- On-the-fly data enrichment and transformation.  
- Integration with business systems for decision support.
