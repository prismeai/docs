# App Store Overview

The Prisme.ai App Store is a central hub where you can discover, install, and manage a wide range of integrations and extensions. In Builder, open any workspace and click **+** beside **Imports** to browse the App Store. These apps expand the platform's capabilities, allowing you to connect to external services, leverage infrastructure components, and enhance your interfaces with specialized functionality.

## App Store Features

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="App Discovery">
    Find the right integrations for your needs:
    
    - **Categorized Browsing**: Explore apps by category, function, or use case
    - **Search Functionality**: Find specific apps based on keywords or capabilities
    - **Detailed Listings**: View comprehensive information about each app
    - **Screenshots and Examples**: See apps in action before installation
    - **User Ratings**: Benefit from feedback from other users
    
    The discovery experience helps you identify the best integrations for your specific requirements.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Installation and Configuration">
    Streamlined process for adding apps to your workspaces:
    
    - **One-Click Installation**: Add apps to your workspace with minimal effort
    - **Guided Setup**: Step-by-step configuration wizards
    - **Secure Credential Management**: Protected storage for authentication details
    - **Version Management**: Control which version of an app is active
    - **Dependency Handling**: Automatic management of required components
    
    The installation process is designed to be straightforward while maintaining security and control.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Usage and Management">
    Efficiently use and manage your installed apps:
    
    - **Integration with Builder**: Seamlessly use app capabilities in workflows
    - **Visibility Controls**: Determine who can see and use each app
    - **Usage Monitoring**: Track how apps are being utilized
    - **Resource Controls**: Manage quotas and limits
    - **Configuration Updates**: Modify settings as needs change
    
    These management features provide governance over your integration landscape.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Development and Extension">
    Extend the App Store with your own apps:
    
    - **Development Framework**: Tools for creating custom apps
    - **Testing Environment**: Validate apps before publication
    - **Private App Store**: Organization-specific app repository
    - **Versioning**: Manage app iterations and updates
    - **Documentation Tools**: Create comprehensive app documentation
    
    These capabilities allow you to build an internal App Store that reflects your organization's unique needs.

</Tabs>
