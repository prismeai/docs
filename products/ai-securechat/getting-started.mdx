This guide will help you start using AI SecureChat, Prisme.ai's secure conversational interface for enterprise AI. Whether you're new to AI or experienced with similar tools, this walkthrough will get you up and running quickly.

## Accessing AI SecureChat

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to Prisme.ai">
    Access your organization's Prisme.ai instance using your credentials.
    
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/securechat-login.png" alt="Prisme.ai Login Screen" />
    </Frame>
    
    If you don't have access credentials, contact your organization's Prisme.ai administrator.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Navigate to AI SecureChat">
    From the Prisme.ai dashboard, select "AI SecureChat" from the main navigation menu.
    
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/navigate-to-securechat.png" alt="Navigating to AI SecureChat" />
    </Frame>
    
    You can also access AI SecureChat directly if it's been set as your default landing page.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start a new conversation">
    When you first enter AI SecureChat, you'll see the main interface with a new conversation ready to begin.
    
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/new-conversation.png" alt="New Conversation in AI SecureChat" />
    </Frame>
    
    You can also access previous conversations from the sidebar if you've used AI SecureChat before.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding the Interface

Let's explore the key elements of the AI SecureChat interface:

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/securechat-interface-labeled.png" alt="AI SecureChat Interface with Labels" />
</Frame>

1. **Conversation Sidebar** - Access and manage your conversations
2. **Message Area** - View the current conversation
3. **Input Box** - Type your messages and questions
4. **Upload Button** - Add documents to your conversation
5. **Canvas Button** - Open the collaborative content creation workspace
6. **Settings Menu** - Access conversation and application settings
7. **Model Selector** - Choose which AI model to use (if multiple are available)
8. **Tools Panel** - Access specialized capabilities (when enabled)

## Your First Conversation

Let's start a basic conversation with AI SecureChat:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Ask a question">
    Type a question or request in the input box and press Enter or click the send button.
    
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/first-question.png" alt="Asking Your First Question" />
    </Frame>
    
    Example questions to get started:
    - "Can you help me draft an email to my team about our upcoming project deadline?"
    - "Summarize the key points about Retrieval Augmented Generation."
    - "What are some best practices for effective virtual meetings?"
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review the response">
    The AI will generate a response based on your question. The response will appear in the message area.
    
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/ai-response.png" alt="AI Response" />
    </Frame>
    
    You'll see the response appear in real-time as it's being generated.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Continue the conversation">
    Follow up with additional questions or requests. AI SecureChat maintains context throughout your conversation.
    
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/follow-up-question.png" alt="Follow-up Question" />
    </Frame>
    
    Example follow-ups:
    - "Can you make that more concise?"
    - "Add a section about [specific topic]."
    - "Rewrite this in a more formal tone."
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Working with Documents

One of AI SecureChat's powerful features is the ability to analyze documents:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload a document">
    Click the upload button (üìé) next to the input box and select a document from your computer.
    
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/upload-document.png" alt="Uploading a Document" />
    </Frame>
    
    Supported formats include PDF, Word, Excel, PowerPoint, text files, and images with text.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Wait for processing">
    The document will be uploaded and processed. This may take a few moments depending on the document size.
    
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/document-processing.png" alt="Document Processing" />
    </Frame>
    
    You'll see a progress indicator while the document is being processed.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Ask questions about the document">
    Once processing is complete, you can ask questions specifically about the document content.
    
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/document-question.png" alt="Asking About a Document" />
    </Frame>
    
    Example document questions:
    - "Summarize the main points of this document."
    - "What are the key findings in the results section?"
    - "Extract the action items from this meeting note."
    - "What data is shown in the table on page 3?"
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Using Canvas for Content Creation

Canvas provides a richer environment for content creation:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Canvas">
    Click the Canvas button in the message input area.
    
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/open-canvas-button.png" alt="Opening Canvas" />
    </Frame>
    
    Canvas will open in a new panel or window, depending on your configuration.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose your workspace">
    Select whether you want to work with text or code.
    
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/canvas-workspace-selection.png" alt="Selecting Canvas Workspace" />
    </Frame>
    
    - **Text workspace** is ideal for documents, emails, and general content
    - **Code workspace** is optimized for programming and technical content
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create content">
    Begin typing or paste existing content to start working. Use the formatting tools as needed.
    
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/canvas-content-creation.png" alt="Creating Content in Canvas" />
    </Frame>
    
    As you work, you can access AI assistance through the contextual menu (right-click or select text and use the floating menu).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Use AI assistance">
    Right-click on selected text to access AI capabilities tailored to your content.
    
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/canvas-ai-assistance.png" alt="Canvas AI Assistance Menu" />
    </Frame>
    
    Common assistance options include:
    - Generate or expand content
    - Refine or edit existing text
    - Change tone or style
    - Translate content
    - For code: Generate tests, add comments, optimize, etc.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Export your work">
    When finished, export your content to the desired format using the export button.
    
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/canvas-export.png" alt="Exporting from Canvas" />
    </Frame>
    
    Export options include:
    - Copy to clipboard
    - Download as document
    - Save to conversation
    - For code: Copy as runnable snippet
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="AI response is too long or detailed">
    - Ask for a more concise response
    - Specify the desired length or format
    - Use phrases like "Summarize in 3 bullet points" or "Keep it under 100 words"
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="AI response is not relevant to my question">
    - Rephrase your question with more specific details
    - Break complex questions into smaller, clearer parts
    - Provide more context about what you're trying to accomplish
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Document processing is taking too long">
    - Check that your document is not excessively large (>100 pages)
    - Ensure the document format is supported
    - Try splitting very large documents into smaller chunks
    - Check your network connection
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="AI keeps referencing outdated information">
    - Remember that the AI's knowledge has a cutoff date
    - Provide current information as context in your question
    - Upload recent documents if you need analysis of current data
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
