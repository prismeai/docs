## Products by User Role

Prisme.ai caters to different user roles within your organization:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="End Users">
    End users primarily interact with:

    - **AI SecureChat** for daily AI assistance
    - **AI Store** to discover specialized agents

    These products require no technical expertise and provide immediate value through intuitive interfaces.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Business Users">
    Business users who need to create specialized solutions use:

    - **AI Knowledge** to connect agents to organizational knowledge
    - **AI Collection** to access structured data
    - **AI Insights** to measure impact and performance

    These products offer no-code interfaces that enable business users to create powerful AI solutions without technical expertise.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Developers">
    Technical users who build advanced solutions leverage:

    - **AI Builder** for custom application development
    - **API integrations** across all products
    - **DSUL** for declarative configuration

    These tools provide the flexibility and power needed for complex, integrated AI applications.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="IT & Administrators">
    IT teams and administrators rely on:

    - **AI Governance** for security and compliance
    - **Deployment options** for infrastructure management
    - **User management** across all products

    These capabilities ensure enterprise-grade security, scalability, and control.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Product Integration

While each Prisme.ai product serves a specific purpose, they are designed to work together seamlessly. Here's how they integrate:

### Common Integration Patterns

1. **Knowledge-Powered Agents**
   - Create specialized knowledge bases in AI Knowledge
   - Deploy them as agents through the AI Store
   - Access through AI SecureChat
2. **Data-Driven Solutions**
   - Connect to structured data with AI Collection
   - Build custom interfaces with AI Builder
   - Track performance with AI Insights
3. **Enterprise Deployment**
   - Manage access with AI Governance
   - Deploy agents through the AI Store
   - Monitor usage with AI Insights
