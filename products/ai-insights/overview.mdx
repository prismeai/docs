## Core Capabilities

### Conversation Ingestion

AI Insights provides multiple options for bringing conversation data into the platform:


- Direct integration with AI Knowledge and AI SecureChat
- Import customer service transcripts from multiple channels
- Process recorded calls with automatic transcription
- Support for chat logs, emails, and messaging platforms
- Automatic metadata extraction and organization

### Custom Evaluation Frameworks

Define and apply tailored evaluation criteria to your conversations:


- Create custom evaluation criteria using natural language
- Build multi-dimensional scoring systems
- Apply different frameworks for different conversation types
- Track performance against key metrics over time
- Benchmark against industry standards or internal goals

### AI-Powered Continuous Monitoring

Leverage AI to monitor conversations at scale:

#### LLM-as-Judge Capabilities
- Apply advanced language models to evaluate conversation quality
- Identify potential compliance issues automatically
- Flag conversations for human review based on risk factors
- Provide explanations for evaluation decisions
- Continuously improve monitoring accuracy through feedback

#### For High-Volume Scenarios
- Intelligent sampling to focus human review where it matters most
- Pattern recognition to identify recurring issues
- Anomaly detection for unusual conversation patterns
- Prioritization based on risk scoring and business impact

### Compliance and Governance

AI Insights includes robust tools for maintaining regulatory compliance:

- Role-based access controls for conversation data
- Audit trails for all review activities
- Automated detection of sensitive information
- Configurable retention policies
- Export capabilities for regulatory reporting
- Sampling methodologies that meet compliance requirements

### Integration with Other Prisme.ai Products

AI Insights works seamlessly with other Prisme.ai products:

- **AI Knowledge** - Monitor knowledge agent conversations
- **AI SecureChat** - Analyze user interactions with conversational AI
- **AI Collection** - Export insights as structured data for deeper analysis
- **AI Governance** - Apply security policies and access controls
- **AI Store** - Monitor performance of deployed AI agents

## Getting Started with AI Insights

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access AI Insights">
    Log in to your Prisme.ai instance and select AI Insights from the main menu.

  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Connect data sources">
    Set up connections to your conversation sources.
    
    You can connect to AI assistants, customer service platforms, or import conversation files.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Define evaluation criteria">
    Create custom evaluation frameworks to apply to your conversations.
    
    
    Specify what you want to measure in natural language, and AI Insights will create the framework.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure monitoring">
    Set up automated or human review processes for your conversations.

    Choose between AI-powered continuous monitoring, random sampling, or targeted reviews.
  </Step>
</Steps>
